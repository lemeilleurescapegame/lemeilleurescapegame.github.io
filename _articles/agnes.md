    When they say follow your dreams, perhaps they don't actually mean that you should follow your nightmares. Not that you had a choice. From the moment you got hold of the necklace, the obsession began. The woman haunting your nightmares was now staring at you from the portrait you found hidden in your great grandmother's chest. You barely remember booking the tickets to Massachusetts, alerting the group of researchers and arriving together in the small town of Willow Creek, known once as SalemThe citizens were adamant about the house of the Blacks being cursed... Many lives were lost behind its dark gates. However, that didn't stop you. You never really believed in nonsense like curses and witches. One of the main reasons you came here in the first place, to prove it first and foremost to yourself. Holding onto the necklace to guide you, surrounded by the blue light of the blood moon, you are more than ready to face the woman disturbing your dreams. Are you ready to face the past? Agnes awaits you.

    The eternal search for the most mythical of treasures. The ambition and the danger surrounding the unravelling of the greatest conspiracy in human history, set up with a single purpose : to protect the location of the most legendary object. Countless scriptures and rumours, verses and historical testimonies, occult sciences and cosmic conspiracies, all coming to the conclusion that the ultimate knowledge demands as its price the ultimate good... You are the new members of a secret team investigating and locating historical treasures and objects of value. It seems that your predecessors mysteriously disappeared during an investigation, but no one can confirm it. With the last address they were operating from as their only clues, and a strange ally "on the inside", it is now your responsibility to find out what happened to them and to continue their investigation...

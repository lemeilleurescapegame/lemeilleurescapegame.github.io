    In this Experimental Program, you have come voluntarily and are invited to participate as experimental animals / samples in an experiment that will test your abilities and your limits, ignoring how, what or why you are actually in this test... The Research and Science Company “Dappart Corporation” welcomes you to the 10th Experimental Program for the Study of Human Skills carried out under the supervision of the Research & Science Center, with state co-financing. Who knows; Maybe at some point you will be able to find out what is behind all this... The physical and mental abilities of the team in problem solving and decision making will be recorded and studied, under the pressure of time. Safety measures have been taken, however, without ruling out the possibility of a possible death. For this reason, the teams that will show rivalry, cooperation and teamwork and will be able to complete their mission are rewarded. The remuneration concerns only the survivors. Differences from previous programs appear around risk, kinesiology requirements and difficulty in solving problems. Applications from heightphobic, acrophobic and claustrophobic will not be accepted. Clothing requirements include comfortable clothing and sneakers.

info_top_en : |
    This room was ranked 22nd in the 2022 TERPECA. What the hell ? Of course, the space is huge and the number of room is impressive. There are fewer padlocks compared to most of the Athenian rooms. Finally, the intro briefing immediately sets the mood, thanks to the gloomy reception area and the acting of the game-master we had. But the rest of the game didn’t convince us. There is too many similar actors interventions. It felt like we had seen the same jump scare over and over like in a bad horror movie. The sets, costumes and masks lack credibility. The extended duration of the room (2h25) was not necessary for us because it does not provide anything additional. Above all, don’t listen to the game master’s advice which would be to run as soon as you see the monster. This is exactly how you risk hurting yourself, by hitting a wall, an object, a partner. And watch where you step so you don’t fall. Even in darkness or dense smoke! Otherwise the fall could have serious consequences. Finally, let’s mention an unrivaled level of sound intensity: earplugs essential.

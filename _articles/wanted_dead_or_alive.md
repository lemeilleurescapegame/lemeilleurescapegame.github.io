info_top_en : |
    This escape room is incredible because the game-master/actor is incredible. This is no longer a secret : the management of No Exit carefully picks its actors and trains them generously. The actor of our game was completely crazy. He could play all the roles, impersonate accents from any region of the world and also make hilarious facial expressions. He was so talented in terms of improvising. We gradually forgot that we were solving puzzles because he was doing everything to entertain us. The actions are integrated into the story and in particular the search which is carried out in conjunction with the actor. A true parody of western style movies.
